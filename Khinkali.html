About Khinkali

Khinkali Recipe

Ingredients: Ground meat, onion, salt, pepper.

Instructions: Mix ground meat, onion, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Fill dumpling wrappers with meat mixture, folding them into a traditional khinkali shape. Boil khinkali in salted water until they float (about 8-10 minutes). Serve hot with your favorite sauce or dipping.
