Acharuli Khachapuri

Recipe:

Mix yeast, sugar, warm water. Let it froth. Combine flour, salt, then add yeast mixture to form dough. Knead, let rise for 1 hour. Roll dough into boat shape. Fill with cheese and crack an egg in the center. Bake at 450째F (230째C) for 10-15 mins. Top with butter and serve hot.
