Mtsvadi

Recipe

Cut the meat into large chunks, about 2 inches in size. Marinate the meat with sliced onions, minced garlic, salt, pepper, and a splash of red wine vinegar or pomegranate juice (if using). Let it marinate for at least 2 hours, preferably overnight. Thread the marinated meat onto skewers, leaving a little space between each piece. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Grill the skewers, turning occasionally, until the meat is cooked through and slightly charred on the outside, about 10-15 minutes. Serve the Mtsvadi hot with your favorite side dishes and enjoy! This simple recipe will give you deliciously tender and flavorful Georgian barbecue
